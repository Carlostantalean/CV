Carlos Guillermo Tantaleán Montoya

He was born in Arequipa, Peru on December 02, 2002. His parents are Francisco Tantaleán and Maricela Montoya. He studied at San Jeronimo School from the age of 3 until he finished high school. Later he studied Computer Science at the Universidad Católica San Pablo and Music at the Universidad Nacional de San Agustín de Arequipa.

During his university years he competed in several programming contests both nationally and internationally, as well as in music contests. In both he managed to stand out notably obtaining several prizes throughout his university life. He was able to make several exchanges in which he had the opportunity to travel to the United States to study Science Computing and in turn was able to continue with both careers both in person and virutal way.

After finishing college he traveled to several countries thanks to various scholarships and recommendations from professors in the United States, which allowed him to do his master's degree in Paris, France, as well as travel to Brazil and India to continue his studies and complete his doctoral studies in computing. Throughout his life he has dedicated himself to carry out various projects to help and support people who lacked education in technology as well as to support music in his country.
